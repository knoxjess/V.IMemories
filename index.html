About Me

"Photography is my way of capturing the beauty of the world."

Hi there! I'm Maria Valencia, and I'm proud to call the picturesque Eagle Mountain, Utah, my home base.

My lens is my creative tool, and it captures the breathtaking beauty of Utah and Colorado, but my true inspiration comes from my three beloved "baby bears."

Beyond photography, I'm an avid artist. Painting, drawing, and photography are not just my professions but also my passions. They provide a canvas for me to express myself and share the wonders I see through my creative lens.

Join me on this artistic journey as I continue to capture the essence of Utah and Colorado, fueled by my devotion to the visual arts and inspired by the love of my little cubs.
